Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 10, 2026

OptionsBot ("the Application") is a private, personal-use trading alert tool operated by a single individual (the "Operator") for their own use. This Privacy Policy describes how the Application handles information.

1. Scope

The Application is not a public service. It sends automated SMS notifications only to phone numbers owned and controlled by the Operator. It does not have users other than the Operator, does not accept sign-ups, and does not provide any service to third parties.

2. Information Collected

The Application does not collect personal information from any third party. The only phone number used by the Application is the Operator's own number, which the Operator has configured directly. No contact lists are imported, purchased, rented, or otherwise obtained.

3. Use of Information

The Operator's own phone number is used solely to deliver trading alerts and operational notifications generated by the Application to the Operator. It is not used for marketing, not shared with third parties, and not used for any purpose other than delivering these alerts.

4. Message Content

Messages sent by the Application contain market data, trade signals, and operational status information generated by the Operator's own software. No message content originates from or concerns any third party.

5. Third-Party Service Providers

The Application uses Twilio, Inc. as its SMS delivery provider. Twilio processes the Operator's own phone number and the message content solely for the purpose of delivering messages. Twilio's handling of this data is governed by Twilio's own privacy policy.

6. Data Retention

The Application retains message logs locally on the Operator's own machine for debugging and audit purposes. These logs are not shared with anyone.

7. Security

The Application runs on the Operator's own hardware. API credentials are stored locally and are not transmitted except to the relevant service providers (e.g., Twilio) over standard TLS-encrypted connections.

8. Children

The Application is not directed to children and does not knowingly process information from children.

9. Changes

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. The effective date above will reflect the most recent revision.

10. Contact

For questions about this policy, contact the Operator directly. The Application is not a public service and does not maintain a public support channel.
